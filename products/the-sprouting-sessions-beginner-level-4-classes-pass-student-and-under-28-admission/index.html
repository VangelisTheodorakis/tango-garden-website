About The Sprouting Sessions

The Sprouting Sessions is our beginner-level Argentine Tango course — 10 weekly classes that take you from zero to dancing with real confidence. No partner needed at any point.

The 4-Class Pass lets you join for a rolling 4-week window within the course. This discounted rate applies to currently enrolled students or anyone 28 years old or younger.

What's Included

  • 4 x 1.5-hour classes on consecutive Tuesdays
  • Partner rotation throughout every session
  • Role of your choice: Leader, Follower, or Double-Role
  • Course runs: 15 April – 17 June 2026

Eligibility

For currently enrolled students (any institution) or anyone who is 28 years old or younger. No proof required — we trust you.

Rolling Cohort Windows

Your pass covers any 4 consecutive Tuesdays within the course period. Message us and we'll match you with the best available window.
